About Me

Aisha Braimoh

I didn't start out in traditional product design. I studied interior design, which probably explains why I'm quite detail-focused and always thinking about structure, flow, and how people move through spaces.

Over time, that shifted into digital products, where I found myself drawn to content-heavy, complex systems especially in financial services. A lot of my work is about making things clearer, more accessible, and easier to navigate.

How I Work

A practical,
people-first approach.

I'm comfortable working in Agile environments and collaborating closely with product owners and engineers to move things forward without overcomplicating the process.

  • 01 Start with understanding real user behaviour not assumptions.
  • 02 Break down complexity into something structured and navigable.
  • 03 Test early, iterate quickly and ship the right thing, not the perfect thing.
  • 04 Keep accessibility as a baseline, not an afterthought.
